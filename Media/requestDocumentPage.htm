Help text

How to use this service

This service displays a list of documents you are able to request. You can request one document at a time. For your records, you are given a receipt number for each request you make.

Information contained in documents you request will not include any provisional information that may have been provided, but not applied to your record.

What type of documents can I request?

This service will allow you to request the following documents for yourself or your partner (if applicable):

  • Detailed Income and Assets Statement;
  • Income Management Statement;
  • Income Statement;
  • Statement of debt.

This service will allow you to request the following documents for yourself (if applicable):

  • Application for Payment form;
  • Centrelink Statement;
  • Deduction Statement;
  • Payment Summary;
  • Rent Certificate;
  • Replacement Concession Card (provided you are currently entitled);
  • Reporting Statement.

Further information about the document types

Application for Payment form

This form can be used if you are required to report your employment income, participation activity and any other changes in circumstances in order to receive your payment. When reporting via the Application for Payment form, you must contact us on your reporting day in order to receive payment.

Centrelink Statement

Your Centrelink Statement gives you information about your payments, income and other details. You need to let us know immediately if any of these details are incorrect or have changed. This will ensure that you are receiving your correct entitlement. Note: We may only disclose personal information to the person the information is about. If you have a partner, any financial investments or property that your partner wholly owns will not appear on your Centrelink Statement.

Deduction Statement

The Deduction Statement gives you information about your deductions from your payments to help you manage your finances. It shows the payment/s you are due to receive, the amounts due to be deducted and the balance remaining. Any planned future deductions or changes are also included. You need to let us know if any of the details are incorrect. Note: We may only disclose personal information to the person the information is about. If you have a partner, any deductions your partner has will not appear on your Deduction Statement

Income Management Statement

The Income Management statement provides you with details of your Income Management account. The statement provides information including Income Management Balance, BasicsCard Balance (if you have one), Income Management BasicsCard, Centrepay and Rent Deductions Scheme regular expenses, Transaction details for Income Management and BasicsCard and Transaction details of recent unpaid Income Management expenses.

Income Statement

If you are applying for government housing assistance or living in government accommodation, you may be asked by your Housing Authority (HA) to provide information about your income and payment details.
This enables the Housing Authority to determine your entitlement to government housing assistance and rent subsidy.
The Income Statement contains previous and future entitlements and payments (regular and irregular), Deductions, and Income and Assets.
An Income Statement can be requested and may be useful for your information or to verify your details with another organisation when applying for concessions or services. The statement contains information about your payments, income and assets that we have recorded.

Reporting Statement

This form can be used if you are required to report your employment income and any other changes in circumstances in order to receive your payment. When reporting via the Reporting Statement, you must contact us on your reporting day in order to receive payment.

Statement of debt

You can request a Statement of debt with any start date, however the maximum period is 5 years within a single request. You can make multiple requests if you need to request a Statement of debt for a period longer than 5 years. You will receive one statement per request. You can request a statement up to todays date, but any future date will not be accepted.

Authorising permission to enquire?

Where your partner has not authorised you to enquire about their personal information, the option to receive their documents will not be available in this service. To arrange authorisation your partner will need to contact us.

What date would you like to request an Income Statement for?

This can be for any date back to the 1st of July of the previous financial year. You can use todays date, but any future date will not be accepted.

What date would you like to request this Statement of debt to begin?

You can request a Statement of debt with any start date in the past, however the maximum period is 5 years within a single request. You can make multiple requests if you need to request a Statement of debt for a period longer than 5 years.

What date would you like to request this Statement of debt to end?

The end date for your request should be within 5 years of the start date and cannot be a future date. The maximum period is 5 years within a single request. You can make multiple requests if you need to request a Statement of debt for a period longer than 5 years.

When can I request a Replacement Concession Card?

A replacement concession card can be requested if your card is:

  • Destroyed
  • Lost
  • Not Received
  • Stolen
  • Or if you need an additional card

You cannot request a replacement if your card is due to expire or be renewed within 14 calendar days.

How long will it take for my card to arrive?

It will take up to ten business days to receive your card via mail. The same card cannot be requested via mail again within this timeframe.

Cards are sent to either your most recent postal address or another address previously specified by you.

Documents requested immediately (.PDF)

Some documents can be requested immediately from us as a PDF (Portable Document Format).

To view, print and save a PDF document you will need an Adobe Acrobat plugin, which can be downloaded for free from the Adobe website.

Documents you request are also available as online letters.

How can I view online letters?

The myGov Inbox allows you to receive letters online securely from Medicare, Centrelink and Child Support services. This makes it easier for you to access information, when you need it. An email or SMS notification will let you know when there are new letters in your Inbox.
