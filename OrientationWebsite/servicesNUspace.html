Watt Space Gallery

Contemporary Art Space

The Watt Space Gallery provides a assortment of exhibits from contemporary art and craft to cultural works.

Northumberland House Cnr King and Auckland Streets, Newcastle NSW 2300

Saturdays - 12 to 4PM

General Enquiries – wattspacegallery@newcastle.edu.au, 4921 8733

The Conservatorium

Music Concert Hall

The Conservatorium is a facility designed to host concerts and music artists from around the world.

As well as teaching instrument playing, the conservatory offers students the chance to become musicians themselves.

Corner of Laman and Auckland streets Newcastle, NSW, Australia 2300

Monday to Friday - 8:30AM to 4:30PM


General enquiries - conservatorium@newcastle.edu.au, +61 2 492 18900


The Forum

Health & Wellness Centre

The Forum is a multipurpose gymnasium, which offers personal training, fitness programs and swimming activities to students at Newcastle University.

5 Workshop Way, Honeysuckle NSW 2300

Mondays to Friday - 5:30AM to 9PM

Saturday to Sunday - 7AM to 6PM


General enquiries - info@theforum.org.au, 02 4921 7001

Hunter St &, Auckland St, Newcastle NSW 2300
