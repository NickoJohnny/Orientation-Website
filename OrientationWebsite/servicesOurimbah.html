Ourimbah Services

A list of all services provided by or via the university

Your Gym

Gym & Exercise Centre

At Ourimbah Campus, Your Gym offers Cardio, Strength, and Fitness classes to university students.


Currently Closed due to COVID

Exercise & Sport Science Building, Ourimbah Campus


General Enquiries - yourgym@yourimbah.com.au, 4348 4194

The Shop

Stationery & Merchandise Store

The Shop is a University operated store located within Ourimbah Campus where you can get all your merchandise and stationary needs.

Student Amenities Building, Ourimbah Campus


Monday to Friday 9AM - 3PM


Ourimbah Library

Library & Study Space

Ourimbah's Library provides numerous services for students from information centres with computers and printers to a MakerSpace where students can learn more about utilising technology for creative reasons and problem solving.

Administration Building, Ourimbah NSW 2258


General Enquiries - +61 (0)2 4348 4020


Ganda Burung

Food Café

Ganda Burung is known as the food shelter which provides University students with a place to eat and talk with others.

SA-136, Student Amenities building


Monday to Friday 8:00am - 2:00pm

Yara Ngura

Library Café

Outside the Oruimbah library is Yara Ngura, a coffee and meal shop known as the Gumtree place.

IRC-101a, Ourimbah Library


Monday to Friday 8AM - 3PM

Food Assistance

The Newcastle Students' Association (UNSA) has your needs in mind with their Food Pantry An initiative aiming to provide food to those in need, Do not hesitate to donate items you no longer require.

Shop101, Ourimbah NSW 2258

Phone - (02)4348 4260
